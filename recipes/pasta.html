pasta firfir

pasta firfir is a delicious Ethiopian pasta dish that is made with pasta, berbere spice mix, and niter kibbeh (Ethiopian spiced butter). It is a great way to use up leftover pasta and is perfect for a quick and easy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine works well)
  • 2 tablespoons niter kibbeh (Ethiopian spiced butter)
  • 1 tablespoon berbere spice mix
  • Salt to taste
